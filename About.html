ACTIVITIES IN OUR RESORT.

Our NCW RESORT offer a diverse range of things to do and facilities to enjoy such as restaurants, bars, coffee lounges, health clubs, workout classes, steam room, sauna, Jacuzzi, spas and swimming pools plus lots of cosy spaces to just hang out and enjoy hotel life.
We also have a conference centre and various event venues in both Nairobi and Eldoret, able to host from 6 to 300 people. Our banquet halls and meeting rooms are ideal for both corporate and social events such as weddings, parties, workshops, exhibitions, cocktails, dinners, business functions etc.
Truly, Kenya is a country with incredible scenery for photo safaris and bucket list ticks. So, get up close and personal with the local wildlife on the savannah, cruise around national parks, meet the Big Five, sponsor an orphaned baby elephant, feed a giraffe, go hiking in craggy mountains or arid deserts, cool off in waterfalls, explore lush forests, swim in the Indian Ocean, bargain for treasures at the local markets and, inevitably, you will fall in love with our country, like so many visitors before you.
